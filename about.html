Our Story

From One Van
To Paris's Finest
Transfer Fleet

Group Blue Van Paris was founded in 2014 with a single blue van and a simple promise: every passenger deserves a stress-free, punctual, and affordable airport transfer. What started as a small family-run taxi transfer operation serving Charles de Gaulle has grown into one of Paris's most trusted private airport transfer services — covering CDG, Orly, Disneyland Paris, and long-distance routes across France and beyond.

Today our fleet of airport cars, executive SUVs, and airport Vehicles handles thousands of transfers per year — yet every booking still receives the same personal attention and fixed, transparent pricing we've offered from day one.
